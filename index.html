Institutional Training

We provide training to college students in communication skills and other soft skills, which will help them to get the best and right employment opportunity.

We offer Institutional Training to the Students of Engineering, Science, Arts, Polytechnic and ITI students in their place of study and in our Training Institute. We conduct some special modules on personality development and counseling right from the first year of their study. We offer the following modules to the Student community.

  1. Soft Skills.
  2. Communication Techniques.
  3. Interview and Group discussion skills.
  4. Life Skills.

We counsel the students individually and in group to keep them in right track. At the end of session mock interviews and Group Discussions will be conducted by the persons working in the same field. Timely Training, Guidance and Counseling to students have proved that they were better placed with good companies.

Placements

We provide Placement services to our clients. After understanding and analyzing our Client requirements, we provide the manpower from our registered Job Seekers well trained by our expert faculty.

We evaluate interest, skills and talent of our Job seeker candidates and we will guide them to the suitable placement in their area of interest. We assure that our special module on "Building positive personality" definitely produces the Positive attitude employee which is the key ingredient of success for any Organization.

Our team members had a track record as Chairman and Members of Interview Committee in various Public Sectors in connection with the recruitment and promotional interviews. We put all our efforts in ensuring maximum customer delight.
